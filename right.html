ABES ENGINEERING LIBRARY

Here's information about the ABES Engineering College library:

Introduction
The ABES Engineering College library, located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India, serves as the knowledge hub of the institution. It provides students, faculty, and staff with access to a vast collection of resources, facilitating academic excellence and research.
Infrastructure
The library spans over 3,500 square meters, with a seating capacity of over 500 individuals. It is fully air-conditioned, providing a comfortable learning environment. The library is divided into different sections, including a reading hall, reference section, journal section, digital library, and reprographic facilities.
Collections
The library boasts an impressive collection of: - Over 1 lakh (100,000) volumes of books - More than 5,000 CDs and DVDs - Subscription to over 300 print journals and 10,000 e-journals - Access to online databases like IEEE, Springer, and ScienceDirect - A vast repository of theses, dissertations, and project reports
Services
The library offers various services, including: - Circulation services (issue and return of books) - Reference and research assistance - Interlibrary loan facilities - Photocopying and printing services - Digital library access - Information literacy programs - Book bank facilities for students
Digital Resources
The library provides online access to various digital resources, including: - Online databases (J-Gate, IEEE, ScienceDirect) - E-books and e-journals - Open-source resources (DOAJ, Directory of Open Access Journals) - Institutional repository (IR)
Operating Hours
The library operates from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekends.
