Welcome to ABES Engineering College

Department of Computer Science

The Department came into inception from year 2018-19 with an intake of 60 seats as B.Tech CEIT (Computer Engineering & Information Technology) branch. Later on in the year 2019-20, two more AICTE approved streams such as Computer Engineering (60 seats) and Computer Science (60 seats) were introduced in the department, in addition to CEIT. Now, from 2020-21 onwards the Department of Computer Science is running with an intake of 180 seats.It has become the most sought after department among engineering aspirants. The department has a team of experienced, highly qualified and motivated faculty members.
